Hardware Guides

Since there are a lot of different pieces of hardware around campus to maintain, it's important to keep track of certain guides for them all.

When a non-classroom computer needs to be swapped, you will need to make sure of a few things.
  1. Do they need it? Make sure that there is a reason for swapping a client's computer.
  2. Do they know? Make sure that there has been contact with the client.
  3. Are you prepared? Make sure that you have a form of external storage to move their data from their old device to their new device.

With the new computer, you will need to make sure it has its own name and is on the university's network. Make sure that all of this information can be easily tracked down for record purposes.
